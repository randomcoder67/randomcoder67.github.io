# Riding All Buses

Inspired by [Geoff Marshall's Excellent Recent Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5Vz3bALCJAs) where he documents riding all of London's bus routes, I decided to try this for my local cities. 

## Rules

* Night Buses, Mobility Buses and School Buses are not included.
	* The reason for exluding night buses is simply that I do not live in a city, so riding night buses would be very difficult (also I don't want to get stabbed)
	* Getting on lots of school buses would make you look like your up to no good
	* Mobility buses I'd just be getting in the way
* A bus route counts when you have gone at least 1 stop on the route. It is not necessary to ride the full route
* Not a strict rule, but I will try to avoid just going back and fourth on one street
	* For example in Edinburgh, you could most likely tick off over half the buses by riding up and down Princes Street for a day, but this is rather boring
* Tourist Buses are not included
* Long range coaches are not included
	* For example in Edinburgh, coaches that go to Glasgow, Dundee, Glenrothes etc. 
	* I will however include airport-shuttle services, such as Edinburgh Airlink
* Trams are not included
* I'm not making this a strict rule, but a bonus challenge could be to ride a portion of every bus route, that is **unique** to that route. I will mark which buses I achieved this for, in case I want to come back to the challenge later
